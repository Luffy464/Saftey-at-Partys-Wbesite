Before the Party: Preparation and Planning

Here are some tips to ensure safety and enjoyment:

  • . Make a plan on how to leave the party safley if you feel unsafe/uncomfortable. Remember, if you feel unsafe it is your body telling you that something is not right, so trust your gut and leave in a situation that makes you feel uncomfortable.
  • . Communicate with your friends about plans and safety measures and tell them what activities you are willing to do with them and what ones you are not because they may make you feel unsafe.
  • . Know the venue: Familiarize yourself with the location and exits so that you are able to leave the party if something/someone makes you feel uncomfortable.

Staying Safe During the Party

Here are some essential safety tips:

  • . Keep an eye on your drink/food and don't accept food or drinks from strangers.
  • . Avoid alcohol or other substances or be aware of your limits.
  • . Stay with friends and check in with each other regularly.
  • . Have a plan for getting home safely.
  • . Avoid risky situations and know when to leave.
  • . Trust your instincts; if something feels off, remove yourself from the situation.
  • . Use the buddy system: always make sure your friends look out for you and you look out for them, just incase something bad happens to you.
  • . Always remember to stay hydrated.

Peer Pressure Strategies

Positive Activities and Fun Choices

Here are some fun ways to enjoy yourself at a party:

  • . Organize games or contests.
  • . Set up a photo booth for fun pictures.
  • . Plan a dance-off or karaoke session.
  • . Offer non-alcoholic drink options and mocktails.

What If Situations

Consider these scenarios and how to handle them:

  • . What if someone at the party has pinned you onto the floor and is about to force you to drink alchohol and you do not want to, what would you do?
  • . Well firstly, you will actually want to not even get yourself into that situation, but how? Well if you have tried saying no and you can see it is not working then leave the party.
  • . However if that fails and you are in that situation, this is when the buddy system may come in handy, you should scream to your freind for help so that you can get out of this sticky situation.
